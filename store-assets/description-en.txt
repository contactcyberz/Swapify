🔄 SWAPIFY — Trade your talents, enrich your life

Can you cook? Play guitar? Code? Do yoga?
Someone near you wants to learn exactly what you know how to do.

Swapify connects people who want to exchange skills — without money. It's simple: you offer 1 hour of your time, you get 1 hour back. We call it timebanking.

─────────────────────────────
🌟 HOW DOES IT WORK?
─────────────────────────────
1. Create your profile and add your skills
2. Discover members near you
3. Propose an exchange in seconds
4. Chat, plan and complete the exchange
5. Rate each other and earn time credits!

─────────────────────────────

─────────────────────────────

💼 Business — Accounting, marketing, finance
